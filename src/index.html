Simple &
transparent
process

Dash makes it easy to supercharge your business by simply detailing what tasks need to be completed and letting your dedicated project manager handle it from there.

Get Started Today
Step 1

Kickoff

You will be assigned a dedicated Project Manager who will schedule an onboarding meeting to quickly get up to speed with your objectives, priority tasks, brand guidelines and any access credentials required. Access will be provided to a private Slack channel and Clickup workspace to use for collaboration. Your project manager will ensure you understand how to create and prioritize tasks.

Step 2

Create & prioritize tasks

Simply detail the projects, big or small, that you need to get completed and our team will take it from there. Your project manager will assign the necessary team members to complete each task from across the full range of specialties. All the necessary delegation is handled for you, allowing you to focus on the final result.

Step 3

Time approval & tracking

Your project manager will estimate the time required for each of the tasks you submit and request your approval before work commences. This ensures that you have full oversight on the use of subscription hours and are in control of where team resources are being used.

Step 4

Execution

The Dash team will begin working on tasks immediately after being approved. Designers, developers and marketers will contribute to tasks as needed to leverage expertise in each specialty required. As work progresses you will receive updates on the status of each task.

Step 5

Feedback & completion

Your project manager will request feedback while a task is in progress to ensure the direction of the Dash team matches your requirements. Giving you the opportunity to review before the final delivery reduces rework and accelerates completion. Once you have received the items you require you can mark the task complete and rate the quality of the delivery. The goal is always to deliver high quality work in the least amount of time possible.

Find answers to your questions here

001

Why not just hire a full-time designer?

Average base salary of a graphic designer or UX/UI designer in the US, UK or Australia is from $60-160k. On top of that are extra benefits, training and insurance!

It'd take you months to find the right designer for your company.

So you will find that with our web and graphic design subscription services, you're essentially getting a premium in-house design agency for the cost of hiring a single mid-level designer.

Last but not least, you wouldn't be able to “cancel” their employment like you can cancel our web and graphic design subscription services if you don't need any work done in a given month!

002

Is there a limit to how many requests or tasks I can make?

Once you sign up, you have 24/7 access to our premium Clickup board. You will be able to add numerous requests and prioritize them.

Unlike other agencies who will only work on one or two requests at a time depending on the plan you subscribe to, all of our plans enable you to request us to work on multiple tasks at the same time. Our priority is to deliver you the best quality to achieve your business goal on time.

003

What counts as a request?

A request or a task is any to-do you assign us. It can be to design a logo, do revisions to an existing design, do website maintenance, or anything else. A request is anything that requires someone from our team to work on any of your projects.

004

How quickly will I get my designs?

It depends, simple request like designing an email signature can take an hour. However, more complex requests can take longer.

005

Who will I be working with?

You will have a dedicated project manager who will be your main point of contact, and they will oversee the processes, plans and billing. They will also arrange for the day-to-day tasks to be defined, reviewed and delivered on time by the art director, senior designers, senior developers and/or senior SEO specialists assigned to your project.

006

Why do I need a project manager?

When subscribing to our design subscription, you can send us all types of tasks. There will be multiple people to work for your needs. A dedicated project manager, experienced in digital project management, will join your team to oversee, consolidate, and create tasks or creative briefs for our talents to work on. Leave the project management to us and focus on the creative journey with us!

007

How do I request work?

Any plan provides you private Slack channel and a Clickup space. You will have various options for giving us requests, including direct communication through Slack, sharing Google docs or wireframes, or even recording a Loom video brief for those who prefer a non-written format. Then all of requests will be created as tasks in Clickup space.

008

What project management tools do you use?

We use ClickUp for task assignment and for time tracking, so we can invite multiple members of your team to the ClickUp board and manage everything there. We can also join other project management tools that you are already using if you prefer.

009

What if I'm not happy with the results?

Don't worry. We got you covered. We'll continue to revise the design until you're 100% satisfied.

010

What if I run out of design work?

That's absolutely okay if you don't have enough design work to do or have only 2-3 simple requests at the moment.

Cancel your subscription anytime you like and resume whenever you've got more requests.

011

What billing options do you offer and can I cancel anytime?

We only offer credit card payment for now. And yes, you can cancel anytime. There are no hidden fees or penalties for cancelation; the subscription will simply not renew from the following month. You can chat directly to your assigned Project Manager.

012

Can I get a refund if I don't like the service?

As we strive to deliver exceptional quality work, we do not offer refunds. However, we provide unlimited revisions to ensure your utmost satisfaction. Our goal is not just to meet your expectations but to exceed them.

013

Who are the individuals responsible for designing?

This is not a nobody design subscription service. Standing behind the service is Bearplus, award-winning digital agency with more than 10-years experience in the industry. The designers are hand-picked from the team to work for your projects. Our reputation for high-quality design will always supersede our goals for monetary gain.

014

Do you offer training or support for using my website or designs?

Yes, we offer training and support services to help our clients effectively use and manage their websites or design assets.

015

Do you provide website hosting and domain registration?

Yes, we offer website hosting and domain registration services to simplify the process of launching and maintaining your website.

016

Can you provide analytics and reporting for my website or app?

Yes, we can set up analytics and reporting to help track and measure the performance of your website.

017

Can you help with website maintenance and updates?

Yes, we offer ongoing website maintenance and support services to ensure that your website remains up-to-date and secure.

018

What tools do you use?

Most UX/UI requests are designed using Figma. For graphic design, we use Adobe Illustrator Adobe After Effects and Photoshop to design. For web developer, we use Webflow and Hubspot. For AI design, we use Midjourney 6, Stable Diffusion 3 and ChatGPT 4.0

However, we may use alternative programs depending on the request received. You do not have to pay for all these software subscriptions when you are subscribed to any of our plans. If you have other design tools, such as Sketch or Adobe XD, that are similar to Figma, we will be able to adapt it.

019

What region do your design subscription cover?

We not only provide design subscription in the US but also support clients from all around the world, including Australia, Canada, Germany, United Kingdom, France, Japan, Korea, Singapore and Malaysia.
