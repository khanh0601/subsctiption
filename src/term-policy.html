Privacy Policy

Last update: 6th of January, 2024

Introduction

At BearInk, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you subscribe to BearInk services.

Information collected

When you use our service, we collect personal information. The ways we collect it can be broadly categorized into the following:

Personal information

When you register on the services, express an interest in learning more about us or our products and services, take part in activities on the services, or contact us in any other way, you directly give us personal information.

The personal information we collect may include the following:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Contact preference

If you make purchases, we additionally gather the information required to process your payment, including your payment instrument number (such as a credit card number) and the security code linked to your payment instrument.

Usage Information

We gather data on your interactions with the BearInk subscription service, including preferences and settings.

How we use your information

Subscription services

Your personal information is used to provide and maintain your BearInk subscription.

Improvements and updates

We analyze usage data to enhance our services and provide updates to meet your needs.

Communication

We may contact you for subscription-related announcements, updates, or promotional offers.

Data security

Encryption

Your personal and payment information is secured through encryption protocols to prevent unauthorized access.

Access controls:

Access to your personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

Third-Party disclosure

Service providers

We may share necessary information with trusted third-party service providers for subscription fulfillment.

Legal compliance

We disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and users.

Updates to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any significant changes will be communicated through our platform or via email.
